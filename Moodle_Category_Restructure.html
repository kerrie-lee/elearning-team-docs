Moodle Category Restructure
What's changing and why

Monash Medical School · Faculty of Medicine, Nursing and Health Sciences
TL;DR
We're adding two new structures to the BMedSc/MD Moodle category tree. DEV sub-categories within each year level give us a proper home for courses under active development or redevelopment — separate from live student sites. BACKSTAGE is a new program-wide category for shared team infrastructure: sandboxes, testing, staff training, and reusable templates. Course names in DEV follow a consistent format: [DEV] Unit Code & Name - Development Site. Student Support sits outside this structure — its sites are continuously updated, so no year or DEV sub-categories apply there (for now).
Why we're making this change
🔀
Problem

Dev work mixed with live sites

Courses being redesigned or built from scratch sit in the same category as live student-facing sites, making it hard to know what's in-progress vs. ready to go.

🏚️
Problem

No shared team workspace

Sandbox sites, staff training courses, and reusable templates are scattered or non-existent. There's nowhere consistent to test tools or store design assets.

✦
Solution

Clear separation, consistent naming

DEV sub-categories isolate development work per year level. BACKSTAGE centralises team infrastructure. A consistent naming convention makes DEV sites instantly identifiable — even when names are truncated.

Quick reference — what lives where
Year X › 20XX
Live, student-facing course sites for a specific delivery year. Visible to enrolled students.
Year X › DEV
Active development and redevelopment work for that year level. Staff only. Named with [DEV] prefix and Development Site suffix.
BACKSTAGE › LAB
Team sandboxes, tool testing, and staff training courses. Safe to experiment — nothing here is live.
BACKSTAGE › TOOLKIT
Polished, production-ready templates and exemplar course sites for reuse across the program.
Student Support
Continuously updated sites with no year cohort structure. Sits outside the DEV/year framework for now.
